What is XM Rebate?

XM rebate is a cashback program that returns part of your trading cost for every lot traded. The more you trade, the more cashback you earn. This is especially beneficial for scalpers and active traders.

How to Calculate XM Rebate

XM rebate is calculated based on your trading volume (lot size) and rebate rate per lot. Use this calculator to estimate your daily, monthly, and yearly rebate instantly.
